We Are MECL

Meghalaya Energy Corporation Limited (MECL) stands as a pivotal entity in the electricity supply landscape of Meghalaya, ensuring reliable and uninterrupted power distribution across the region. With a comprehensive network of transmission and distribution infrastructure, MECL efficiently delivers electricity to residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional consumers. Through meticulous planning and execution, MECL strives to optimize the distribution grid to minimize transmission losses and enhance the quality of service provided to customers. Moreover, MECL continuously invests in upgrading and modernizing its infrastructure to meet the evolving energy demands of Meghalaya, while also incorporating renewable energy sources to promote sustainability and reduce environmental impact. With a customer-centric approach, MECL remains committed to delivering affordable, accessible, and high-quality electricity services, thus playing a vital role in the socio-economic development and progress of Meghalaya and its residents.
